Chicken Biryani

A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and marinated chicken.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 60 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala)

Equipment

  • Large cooking pot
  • Frying pan
  • Knife and cutting board

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Fry onions until golden, then add chicken.
  3. Cook rice separately, then layer with chicken.

Mutton Karahi

A rich, spicy tomato-based mutton dish cooked in a wok.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 4 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (red chili powder, garam masala, salt)

Equipment

  • Wok or karahi
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Stirring spoon

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add mutton and spices, and cook until tender.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Garnish with green chilies and serve hot.

Gulab Jamun

Delicious deep-fried milk dumplings soaked in sugar syrup.

Serving Size: 8

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Equipment

  • Deep frying pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Syrup pot

Instructions

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee to form a dough.
  2. Make small balls from the dough.
  3. Deep-fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Boil sugar and water to make syrup, then add cardamom powder.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the syrup for 2 hours and serve.
