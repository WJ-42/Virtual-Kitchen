About Pakistani Cuisine:

Pakistani cuisine is known for it's rich spices, slow-cooked meats, and diverse flavors, blending Mughal, Persian, and South Asian influences.

Our History:

Waleed's Dhaba started as a small roadside eatery in Lahore, known for its signature dishes like Biryani and Karahi. Over the years, we’ve grown into a community favorite, bringing authentic flavors to your doorstep.
